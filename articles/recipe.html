Carrot Cake

by Julia Walser

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 t. soda
  • 2 t. baking powder
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • 2 t. cinnamon
  • 1 t. salt
  • 1 1/2 cup oil
  • 4 eggs

Directions:

Mix oil and eggs. Add carrots. Mix dry ingredients and add to carrot mixture. Pour into 3" x 8" greased cake pan or sheet pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.

Cream Cheese Topping

Ingredients:

  • 1 8-oz package of cream cheese
  • 1 stick of unsalted butter
  • 2 t. Pure vanilla extract
  • 1 box confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup chopped nuts

Directions:

Beat and spread on cake. Top with chopped nuts (optional).
