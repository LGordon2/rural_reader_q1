To Greensboro Charity Initiative: Second Harvest Food Bank To Meet the Consultants

Coding Challenge: Season Two

by Lily Powell

The coding challenge has just finished its second season, which ran from April 1st through June 30th. A total of 1,829 submissions were made by 26 participants in this season's challenge. The most active participant for Season 2 was Stan Herring, with 310 submissions. The season's most successful participants were Ethan Bell, Jock Lanier, and Jonathan Doll, each of whom had 2 wins. The following consultants had 5 or more successful submissions for Season 2: Stanley Grooms, Brandon Sims, Jonathan Doll, Jock Lanier, Kevin Munro, and Ethan Bell.

The prizes up for grabs were a PlayStation 4, a Parrot Drone, and an inclusive trip for two to either Carowinds or Six Flags theme parks. Season 2 winners were announced via live webcast and were: Erin Dodd, Brandon Sims, and Jock Lanier. Congratulations to all the winners!
